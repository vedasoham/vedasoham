### About Me
I am a **PhD scholar** at *Gujarat Biotechnology University (GBU)* specializing in structural biology and protein engineering with a focus on protein design, folding, and dynamics. My expertise lies in performing both classical and advanced molecular dynamics (MD) simulations, including steered MD, metadynamics, and umbrella sampling. Additionally, my research involves identifying small-molecule drug candidates to combat WHO-priority multi-drug resistant (MDR) strains.
